Welcoming Bluey

Love, Marriage and a Baby Carriage!

It starts with love which lead to a Texas wedding and then a Georgia baby.

Love

I met my husband when we were in High School. He quickly became my best friend.

We continued to date for 5 years before he proposed!

Marriage

I was currently stationed in Texas for training, but we decided to pick a weekend and finally get married!

We had a small August wedding and spent the weekend enjoying eachothers company.

We spent the first few months of our marriage apart before I got assigned my next duty station in Georgia!

Baby Carriage

Shorty after arriving to Georgia, we found out that I was pregnant with Bluey!

With a pandemic approaching, it was a scary time to have a baby, but our families were very supportive.

Bluey was born in October and has been causing mischief ever since!

...I wouldn't have it any other way.
